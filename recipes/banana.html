Banana Bread Recipe

Prep time:15 mins

Cook time:1 hrs

Total Time:1hrs 15 mins

Yield:1 9x5-inch loaf

Servings:10

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking side
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C)

    Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. Combine bananas, sugar, egg, and butter together in a bowl.

    Mix flour and baking soda together in a seperate bowl

    Stir into banana mixture until batter is just mixed

    Stir in salt; pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven until

    A toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean

    About 1 hour.
