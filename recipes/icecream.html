Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe

Prep Time:5 mins

Cook Time:5 mins

Additional Time:2 hrs 20 mins

Total Time:2 hrs 30 mins

Servings:4

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Stir milk, cream, and sugar in a saucepan over low heat

    until sugar has dissolved. Heat just until the mixture is hot

    and a small ring of foam appears around the edge.

  2. Transfer cream mixture to a pourable container like a large measuring cup

    Stir in vanilla extract and chill mix thoroughly, at least 2 hours.

  3. Pour cold ice cream mix into an ice cream maker, turn on the machine

    churn according to manufacturers directions, 20 to 25 mins.

  4. When ice cream is softly frozen, serve immediately or

    Place a piece of plastic wrap directly on the ice cream

    And place in the freezer to ripen, 2 to 3 hours.
